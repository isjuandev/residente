# Privacy Policy Template

Effective date: `<date>`

Residente provides educational medical algorithms for students and healthcare professionals. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how users can contact us.

## Information We Collect

- Account information: email address and encrypted authentication identifiers.
- Subscription information: plan status and payment provider identifiers.
- Usage information: viewed diseases, favorites, search activity, app version, device type, and diagnostics.
- Offline data: diseases and favorites saved locally on the device.

Residente does not request, store, or process patient medical records.

## How We Use Information

- Provide authentication and account access.
- Sync favorites and offline content.
- Process subscriptions and entitlement checks.
- Improve reliability, performance, and product quality.
- Detect, investigate, and prevent abuse or security issues.

## Analytics And Diagnostics

We use Sentry to collect crash reports, performance traces, device metadata, app version, and technical diagnostics. Expected authentication or validation errors are filtered when possible.

## Payments

Payments are processed by Google Play, Apple App Store, and MercadoPago where applicable. Residente does not store full card details.

## Data Retention

Account data is retained while the account is active. Diagnostic events are retained according to the configured Sentry retention policy. Users may request deletion by contacting support.

## Security

Tokens are stored using platform secure storage. Data in transit is protected with HTTPS.

## Children

Residente is not intended for children under 13.

## Contact

For privacy requests, contact: `privacy@residente.app`.
