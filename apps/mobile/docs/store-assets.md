# Store Assets

## App Icon

- Source: `1024x1024` PNG, no alpha channel, square corners.
- Android generated icons: place under `android/app/src/main/res/mipmap-*`.
- iOS generated icons: place under `ios/Runner/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset`.
- Visual direction: white medical cross or clinical algorithm mark over `#1A5276`.

## Play Store

- Feature graphic: `1024x500` PNG or JPEG.
- Phone screenshots: 2 to 8 images, `1080x1920` or `1440x2560`.
- 7-inch tablet screenshots: optional but recommended, `1200x1920`.
- 10-inch tablet screenshots: optional but recommended, `1600x2560`.
- Short description: max 80 characters.
- Full description: max 4000 characters.

## App Store

- iPhone 6.7-inch screenshots: `1290x2796`.
- iPhone 6.5-inch screenshots: `1242x2688`.
- iPhone 5.5-inch screenshots: `1242x2208`.
- iPad Pro 12.9-inch screenshots: `2048x2732`.
- App preview videos: optional, 15 to 30 seconds.
- Subtitle: max 30 characters.
- Promotional text: max 170 characters.
- Description: max 4000 characters.

## Required Screenshot Set

1. Search and specialty filters.
2. Disease detail with algorithm flowchart.
3. Clinical tables and references.
4. Favorites and offline indicator.
5. PRO upgrade screen.

Avoid showing patient data, real credentials, or screenshots from non-production APIs.
